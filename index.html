Flush was designed with a simple idea in mind. Pass the GA FEWD Course. But the team wanted to have a bit of fun while doing it. The rules of the game are simple enough. Hover over an image to see the quote attributed to this person and earn or lose points when you give a correct answer. Yup! It's that simple! There's no limit to the number of times you can play, so click away!
And most importantly, don't forget to Flush!
