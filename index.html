What's Flush?

Flush was designed with a simple idea in mind. Pass the GA FEWD Course. But have a bit of fun while doing it. The rules of the game are simple enough. Hover over an image to see the quote attributed to this person and earn or lose points when you give a correct answer. Yup! It's that simple! There's no limit to the points you can currently earn, so click away to your heart's content! And don't forget to Flush!
